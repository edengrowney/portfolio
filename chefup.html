Chef Up App

ChefUp is an innovative, AI-powered social platform concept designed to bring cooking enthusiasts together in a dynamic and interactive online space. Developed as a collaborative group project, the idea for ChefUp is supported by comprehensive research and a detailed business plan. The platform aims to cater to a wide range of users, from novice cooks to seasoned chefs, with features like personalized recipe recommendations, a community-driven feed, and tools for managing kitchen activities such as pantry organization and dinner party planning. By leveraging cutting-edge technology, including computer vision for pantry syncing and AI for recipe assistance, ChefUp is poised to bridge the gap between culinary creativity and practical kitchen management. While it has not been developed yet, the project highlights skills in product design, technical planning, and interdisciplinary teamwork.
