Real Time Bar Line Estimator using Computer Vision Techniques

Our project leverages real-time data processing and computer vision to estimate wait times at Rickâ€™s American Cafe, a popular campus bar. Utilizing the YOLOv8 architecture fine-tuned with Roboflow, we developed a system capable of detecting and tracking individuals in video streams. Key techniques included multi-object tracking, spatial-temporal attention, and time-series analysis using regions of interest (ROIs) to analyze crowd dynamics. The model achieved a 95% precision and 100% recall for entry event identification, though challenges remain, such as occlusions, extended queues, and varying environmental conditions. In the future, we aim to refine the model for crowded settings, integrate external data like weather and local events, and enhance prediction accuracy. Ultimately, we plan to transform this into a publicly accessible application, offering real-time wait predictions to improve customer satisfaction and service efficiency.
